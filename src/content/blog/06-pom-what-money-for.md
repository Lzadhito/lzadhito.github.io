## Man In The Car Paradox

While some of us want to buy expensive things just to show or to be recognized by people (including specific people you targetting), the truth is when we see another else with a luxury thing we rarely admiring them in place, instead we imagined ourselves positioned in their condition. For instance, current gen Z might've been having a lot of problem of buying a house, and when we see our gen Z friend having a house, we rarely admire them and instead we imagined if we positioned into the one who had the house.

## Wealth Is What You Don't See

 We often see people with a big house, or luxury car having difficulties in debt. We might also see a big spender can just retain their lifestyle for small period of time. This idea highlights that true wealth is invisible because it is defined by the money people save, invest, and keep not what they spend. Fancy homes, gadgets, and cars show consumption, not wealth. Real wealth is the financial security and optionality created by unspent money, which silently supports freedom and long-term stability.

## Freedom

The highest form of wealth is the ability to control your time. Not only about what you can buy, but to decide what you do, when you do it, and with whom. Most of us probably heard enough about how rich people regret his lost time just to chase money and left their family behind. The hard truth is it probably applied for low economical classes that lost their time just to be survive.

The goal of the money itself is to gain freedom. While in this capitalism era your choice are narrowed to generating and maintaing money, being independence from thinking about it every time gave you capacity to think and do another thing. That's why you need to be strategic with money. You also need to define the freedom (or maybe what freedom) you want to pursue.

For instance the freedom to life elderly life without thinking a lot about money means having pension saving, which you save X time of your monthly spending + emergency fund. The freedom to adjust you career, instrospect about your goals means having career break savings that start from quiting a job/having unpaid leave, spend time besides working, prepare to work again, finding a work, start to work, that really need you a lot of saving. You define what you need, the money you need, you save and plan how much you spend and how you regain.
